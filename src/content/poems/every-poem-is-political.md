poemText: |
  in the sense that everything is political
  even sunflowers

  this blue sky, stands in opposition
  to the air sirens above my birth city

  this deep ocean
  shelters undocumented oil fields

  the great mountains in the distance
  are unapologetically arresting…

  everywhere you look is a reason to protest
  everywhere you look is <em>so beautiful</em>

  never forget

  every tongue is a borderland
  every heart, a homeland worth longing for
