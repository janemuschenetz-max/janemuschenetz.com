about: "#ME TOO renders real-world statistics on sexual violence and prevention into letters through data charts created in Microsoft PowerPoint. The poem is an homage to the ME TOO movement to end SA complicity and victim shaming, which became popularized on social media. The title, a statement of inherent solidarity and consensus, is belied by the poem's subtext, acknowledging SA as a politicized and polarizing issue."
