about: "#ME TOO renders real-world statistics on sexual violence (SV) and prevention into letters through data charts created in Microsoft PowerPoint. The poem is an homage to the ME TOO movement (popularized via social media) to end SV complicity and victim shaming.\n\nThe title, a statement of inherent solidarity and consensus, is belied by the poem's subtext, acknowledging SV as a politicized and polarizing issue. The stark statistics contrast with backlash claims that SV is not a prevalent problem, \"the pendulum has swung too far the other way,\" and that it is only an issue for certain genders or sides of the political spectrum.\n\nThe visual staging of the poem echoes a scientific paper, with boxed and numbered text boxes explicating the data. The pink and blue of the large chart-based letters calls attention to SV's cross-gender impact and perceived acceptance of a chilling status quo."
