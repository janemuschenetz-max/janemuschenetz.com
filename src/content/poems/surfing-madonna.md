poemText: |
  Never once have I crossed myself
  I want to believe that —
  Mercy, no matter our insecurities
  Faith, no matter our flaws
  Hope, for a better world
  Love, for our neighbors, for our whole selves...

  You see, everyday miracles
  (outside of Churches and Temples)
   — we too can walk on water,
  with the right wave beneath us.
