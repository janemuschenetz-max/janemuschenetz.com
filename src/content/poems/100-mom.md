about: "'100% Mom' renders real-world statistics on maternal mortality, unpaid labor, and workforce inequality into letters through charts created in Microsoft PowerPoint. The visual staging of the poem echoes a scientific paper, with boxed and numbered text explicating the data. The simple, almost childlike rhyme scheme and musicality of the text is juxtaposed with the stark statistics. The \"Pepto-bismol\" pink of the large word MOM provides additional dissonance with the subject matter, calling attention to the perceived acceptance of a chilling status quo. 100% MOM won first place in the creative verse single poem category of the 2025 National Federation of Press Women Communications Contest and was nominated for Best of the Net. It was first published in Whale Road Review."
